The story is about Seo Dal Mi, who dreams of becoming the next Steve Jobs, so she opens her own start-up, her first love, Nam Do San, the founder of Samsan Tech, and Han Ji Peng (Kim Seong Ho), the leader of the investment company SH Venture Investment Firm. The plot tells about their dream to create a kind of Silicon Valley in Korea, where companies flock to become the future of Google and Amazon.Won Ying Jae (Kang Han Na) is an executive director with an MBA from Wharton Business School. She has an excellent education, good looks, wealth, the girl quickly developed her own startup. Many believe that she is too young for such a career, but she has worked hard on her current position in society. And she also has a family secret.
